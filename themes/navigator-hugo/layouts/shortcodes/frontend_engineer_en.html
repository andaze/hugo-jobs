Benefits of Working at Vage
  • Because we deal directly with many clients, there are few projects with unreasonable schedules.
  • There are initiatives to acquire more advanced knowledge and skills, such as JavaScript study sessions by external instructors.
  • Receive appropriate feedback from talented senior engineers.
  • Because we are commissioned, we are able to work on a variety of projects for a variety of companies.
  • With marketing as our strength, we can learn the essential engineering that can contribute to business.
  • Exposure to UX research and design thinking approaches
  • Able to develop high quality designs in consultation with in-house designers
  • Can be involved in the development of not only contracted services but also in-house services
  • 140 separate workflows and other elaborate project management know-how.
For example, a job like this
  • Development of web sites using HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • Development of applications using HTML/CSS/JavaScript
  • Implementation of WordPress and CMS
  • Linkage and utilization of various APIs for existing services
  • Server-side development such as PHP
  • Conducting tests
  • Creation of development-related documentation
  • Collaboration with designers
For example, someone like this
  • HTML coders who want to master the front-end
  • Front-end engineer at a business company who wants to experience development in a wide range of industries
  • Front-end engineers who want to be able to run the organization like a CTO.
Basically, we are looking for experienced people only.

Selection Process
  1. document screening
    An initial screening will be made based on your resume and achievements.
  2. first interview
    We will ask you some basic questions about your career plans, including your past work history. We will also answer all of your questions about Vage.
  3. Issue
    You will be asked to submit an assignment for front-end engineers before the second interview.
  4. Second Interview
    Interview with our representative and site manager. We will have a more in-depth discussion, focusing on feedback on the assignment.
  5. Final Interview
    Both parties confirm their final intentions by checking the working conditions and other details.
    The process and content are subject to change depending on the characteristics of the applicant.
Currently, we continue to receive two to three applications per month. If the hiring slots are filled, the recruitment process may be suspended. If you are interested, please apply as soon as possible.
