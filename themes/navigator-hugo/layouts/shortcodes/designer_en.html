  • Our unique education system allows you to acquire a minimum level of knowledge and skills.
  • Because we deal directly with many clients, there are few projects with unreasonable schedules.
  • Excellent senior designers are there to give you appropriate feedback.
  • Because we are commissioned, we are able to work on various designs for various companies.
  • With marketing as our strength, we can learn essential design that can contribute to business.
  • Able to think about design from a UX research and design thinking approach
  • Able to design with technical backing from in-house engineers

Salary

280,000 yen per month (3.36 million yen per year) for both working adults and those with no experience. For those with experience, the approximate range is 4-7 million yen, to be determined based on performance and skills. One twelfth of the annual bar is paid on the 25th of the following month. After joining the company, an assessment will be made at the end of the year based on Bayzi's own evaluation system, and the annual salary for the following year will be decided through discussions.

Selection Process
  1. document screening
    An initial screening will be made based on your resume and achievements.
  2. first interview
    We will ask you some basic questions about your career plans, including your past work history. We will also answer all of your questions about Vage.
  3. Issue
    You will be asked to submit an assignment for designers before the second interview.
  4. Second Interview
    Interview with our representative and site manager. We will have a more in-depth discussion, focusing on feedback on the assignment.
  5. Final Interview
    Both parties confirm their final intentions by checking the working conditions and other details.
    The process and content are subject to change depending on the characteristics of the applicant.
Currently, we continue to receive two to three applications per month. If the hiring slots are filled, the recruitment process may be suspended. If you are interested, please apply as soon as possible.
